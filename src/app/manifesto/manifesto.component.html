The Manifesto

This website is an experiment. Selfishly, I hope it gives me a chance to communicate with you without battling a recommendation algorithm for your attention. I don't think that's good for either of us over the long haul.

This site has no comments, no like buttons, and there's no bot tracking how long you spent on a certain page. This format was elected for a couple reasons: First, I wanted to make my own website from scratch for fun. Second, I wanted a place where I could share stuff without seeing how many likes it did, or more crucially, did not get. Don't get me wrong, I still have social media for more "official" releases (I'm not really beating the system) but I'm excited to see what may happen in this simplified format.

All this in mind, it's pretty impressive that you are reading this and have found my message-in-a-bottle floating in an ever expanding sea of content. Which is to say: I'm glad you are here. If you want to say hi, feel free to shoot me an email at virginiaboymusic@gmail.com. I hope to hear from you :)
