Hello and thank you for checking out out my bio! I'm Robert. I get a lot of joy from connecting different ideas across domains and spend much of my day navel-gazing about the interconnectivity of life. "It's all one big pizza" is my current catch phrase and I'm looking forward to a future with that on a T-shirt.

About me: I graduated from Virginia Tech in 2020 with a degree in Industrial & Systems Engineering where I spent many meaningful hours studying/researching the efficiency of systems. In industry, I've worked in the IT consulting and software startup world as a web developer/engineering generalist. Outside of my passions for coding, mathematics, and well designed algorithms; I write songs and poems. In 2017, I put out my first album, Virginia Boy, and am currently working on my second. With my writing, my goal is to communicate and not just express myself, and I think I'm getting better at that since when I first started.
